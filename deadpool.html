Deadpool 2 at 7-Eleven

Unleash Deadpool in-store and earn 7Rewards points

With the launch of Deadpool 2 in theaters, 7-Eleven and Zappar teamed up to to give 7-Eleven customers a chance to see their favorite anti-hero in full augmented reality glory -- and also a chance to earn 7Rewards points for their shopping!

By accessing the Zappar-powered AR feature in the 7Rewards app, customers could visit their local 7-Eleven store and scan specially-marked codes to earn 7Rewards points and unlock exclusive Deadpool AR games and face filters each week!

With this summer campaign lasting 8 weeks, users were spoilt with a variety of twelve different experiences! From playing games like "Whack-a-Unicorn" and "Mutant Attack" to taking uncomfortable unicorn selfies with Deadpool himself, each week brought something new and exciting to try.

With almost 1 million unique users entering the AR experience, the campaign proved to be a huge success in terms of numbers and engagement!
