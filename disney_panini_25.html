Carrefour Panini 25 DLP

A Disney sticker collection app

In celebration of the 25th Anniversary of Disneyland Paris, Carrefour Belgium, Disney, and Panini all teamed up to bring the Carrefour Panini 25 DLP sticker collection.

What more, Panini teamed up with Zappar to bring the magic of AR to the collection! Use the Carrefour Panini 25 DLP app to scan stickers into your digital collection. Keep an eye out for specially marked AR stickers-- scan them to bring to life some of your favourite Disney characters including Mickey, Minnie, Buzz, and others.
