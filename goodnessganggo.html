Goodness Gang Go Series

A Goodness Gang Collect-a-thon

TCC and Zappar worked in collaboration to bring augmented reality to the Goodness Gang IP. Through the Goodness Gang Go app series, users were able to scan specially marked codes in retail space to collect digital versions of their favourite Goodness Gang characters.

With each newly scanned character, users were able to unlock a fun new game. By playing games and earning points, users were able to spend their rewards on customizing their own in-game avatar and personal garden.

Although originally coined the "Goodness Gang Go" project, the app actually took various forms and names across retail markets in Europe (Vitaminis in Netherlands, Goodness Gang Go in Ireland, Gang Świeżaków in Poland, etc.) For each market, retailers were able to release a unique version of the app tailored for their promotional campaign, each featuring different characters, games, language, and branding.

The app series has proven to be a large success with more than a hundred thousand downloads on the Play and App Store.
