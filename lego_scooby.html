Lego Scooby-Doo

An Augmented Reality, Mystery Machine Dash!

Inside every Lego Scooby-Doo set you'll be able to unlock a new level in this augmented reality racing game. Simply scan the back of the Lego set manual to release the Lego versions of the Scooby Gang!

When you scan, you'll first be greeted by the Mystery Machine riding into view. Choose to play the game and you'll be taken to an overworld map with five spooky levels. Tap on a level to transport the Mystery Machine into a fast-paced mini-game where you must ram the baddies and avoid falling into chasms. The further you travel, the faster and more difficult the game becomes! How much can you score?
