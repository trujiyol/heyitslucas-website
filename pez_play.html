PEZ Play

A PEZ Mini-game Collection Extravaganza

PEZ initially approached Zappar with the task of helping increase candy sales and further drive consumer engagement. The response to the brief came in the form of PEZ Play, an app which allowed consumers to scan PEZ candy codes on inlays and products restore the color back to PEZ World.

With each scan, users are awarded with a magic key that allows access to a new level on the world. Each level offers a different activity, from reaction-based games to fun photo opportunities.

Originally launching with seven games and activities, PEZ Play has grown to house more than twenty activities (and two planets) for users to unlock and enjoy.
