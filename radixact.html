Accuray Radixact: AR & MR Product Visualisation

Product visualisation through mixed reality

Mixed reality can remove the constaints of the physical world on product visualation. During the ESTRO 36 conference in Vienna, Accuray worked alongside Zappar to showcase its trademark Radixact product and give users the ability to actually see inside the machine.

Rendering the machine through an 'x-ray view', Accuray was able to display the interior components of its new technology while showcasing various aspects of treatment. Beyond the MR experience, attendees were also provided AR-enhanced print material that allowed the experience to be taken home and relived on a smaller scale.
