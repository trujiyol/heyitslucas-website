Belgian Red Devils AR sticker book

A World Cup Grand Challenge

Once again, Panini, Carrefour, and Zappar team up to bring the magic of AR to shoppers! This time, insupport of the Belgian Red Devils, they cram all the excitement of the 2018 World Cup in the most jam-packed app of the season! Users are able to create their own avatar as the final member of the team!

Using the app's in-built scanning functionality, users can scan special Red Devils stickers (available in Carrefour Belgium) to power up their avatar and add to their virtual sticker collection! Scanning the RedTogether sticker album cover unlocks the fast-paced Football Runner game that will test their avatar's, and their own, skills on the pitch!

If that wasn't enough, users are able to see their favorite Red Devil's players come to life by scanning specially-marked stickers! From Fellani to Wetsel, see them all appear in full 3D right there on your sticker collection!
