Star Wars at Carrefour Spain

Tilt and turn the Star Wars characters

Enter the world of Star Wars through the "Héroes y Villanos" function in the Mi Carrefour app (Spain), powered by Zappar! Come in-store and spend over 1€ to receive specialy-marked cards! Scan the unique codes on the back of select cards to unlock tilt-driven AR experiences set in the Star Wars universe!

Users play as one of eight memorable Rollinz character. By physically tilting the cards, and using gravity to navigate their character over hologram nodes, players can collect information and complete their favorite character's profile! Can you collect and complete all the cards?

The app generated 22,500 unique users with an average dwell time of 137 seconds, totaling 175 days spent interacting with the AR experiences -- a huge success for a relatively small campaign!
