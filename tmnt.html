Nickelodeon TMNT Retail Hunt

The Turtles Go Globe-trotting!

Through the magic of augmented reality, Toys R Us and Zappar come together to bring this store-wide Turtle scavenger hunt! The turtles have gone on an international tour to explore all the pizzas of the world, and it's up to you to find them all!

Go in-store and look out for the different manhole floor mats to unlock each of the famous team members! Scan to see your favorite turtle standing in front of you. Can you guess which countries they've visited (Hint: just take a look at what they're wearing)? Find all four turtles and unlock a chance to visit their lair and play an exclusive skateboarding game!
