Dreamworks Trolls AR experiences in Tesco

A Magical Trolls Hunt

With the release of the summer blockbuster Trolls in 2016, shoppers were given the chance to head in store to find and collect all the loveable Trolls characters! By scanning unique codes using the Zappar app across various point of sale and merchandise, users would reveal new members of the "Rat Pack" and unlock fun activities with Poppy and friends.

With Poppy by their side, users could help cheer up Branch, paint a colorful bug, watch a movie while sitting on a toadstool, or try on some crazy Trolls hairstyles! Each code scanned also unlocked new photo opportunities with the characters and stickers for a virtual scrapbook!

Through the course of the campaign, over forty thousand unique users took part in the hunt! Partner brands could get involved by using zapcodes on their packaging to be part of the hunt.
