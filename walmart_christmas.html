Santa's Sleigh Ride at Walmart

See the magic of Christmas come to life in-store

Partnering with Advantage Solutions, Zappar helped bring Christmas-themed augmented reality content to Walmart customers for the 2018 holiday season.

Shoppers in 1,000 Walmart stores were able to scan codes and product logos via the Walmart app to unlock exclusive aisle experiences featuring their favourite iconic brands including Pepsico, Welschâ€™s, Kellogg's, Clorox, Nickelodeon, M&M's, Blue Bunny Ice Cream and 3M.

Each aisle experience placed the user in the middle of a magic snow-covered aisle where they were tasked with collecting as many emblems and special products as they could before time ran out. Familiar friends and new faces appear to hand out special gifts -- or give users a face full of snow! At the end of the experience, users were awarded with a fun face filter to try on and share with friends.
