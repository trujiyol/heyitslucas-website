Zapbox Golf

Mixed Reality Golfing

Ever wanted to play a round of virtual mini-golf in your bedroom? Well now you can! With Zapbox Golf users can play a short round of three mini-golf courses all from the comfort of their own home.

This isn't any ordinary virtual golf! By strapping on a VR headset and Zapbox wand, users are fully immersed in a mixed reality game that takes virtual golfing to a new level! With maximum degrees of freedom, users can walk around and freely explore every corner of the course. Users must play their best game in order to unlock each new course -- so aim for that hole-in-one!
